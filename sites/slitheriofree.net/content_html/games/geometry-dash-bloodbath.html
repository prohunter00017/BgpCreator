42-53% (Crack)

54-70% (ASonicMen)

You'll encounter a triple-speed micro-UFO, flying through gears and spikes while adjusting gravity and size. A ball sequence with memory-based gameplay and blue spheres will challenge your focus. This section is perfect for honing your memory and multitasking skills.

71-79% (Etzer)

80-87% (Havok)

93-98% (Ggb0y)

98-100% (Riot)
